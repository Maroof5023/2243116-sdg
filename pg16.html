GOAL 16:

Encapsulated within Goal 16 of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, aims to build societies that are peaceful, just, inclusive, and accountable. This goal recognizes that without strong institutions, respect for the rule of law, and access to justice, sustainable development cannot be achieved.
Goal 16 emphasizes the importance of reducing violence, promoting the rule of law, and ensuring equal access to justice for all. It calls for the establishment of accountable and transparent institutions at all levels of governance. Efforts toward "Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions" include initiatives to prevent and reduce violence, combat corruption, and promote human rights. This goal recognizes the importance of empowering vulnerable populations, ensuring access to justice, and fostering inclusive decision-making processes. Societies plagued by violence, corruption, and unequal access to justice hinder progress and undermine human rights. Goal 16 strives to create an environment where citizens can participate in governance, seek justice, and trust in institutions.
Challenges persist, including conflicts, human rights abuses, and weak governance structures. Achieving Goal 16 requires collective action, political will, and the commitment of governments, civil society, and individuals. By fostering peaceful societies, upholding human rights, and building accountable institutions, societies can lay the foundation for sustainable development and a better future for all.

THE TARGETS

Everyone can help to make sure that we meet the Global Goals. Use these targets to create action to promote peace, justice and strong Institutions.

  • COMBAT ORGANIZED CRIME AND ILLICIT FINANCIAL AND ARMS FLOWS
    By 2030, significantly reduce illicit financial and arms flows, strengthen the recovery and return of stolen assets and combat all forms of organized crime.
  • STRENGTHEN THE PARTICIPATION IN GLOBAL GOVERNANCE
    Broaden and strengthen the participation of developing countries in the institutions of global governance.
  • ENSURE PUBLIC ACCESS TO INFORMATION AND PROTECT FUNDAMENTAL FREEDOMS
    Ensure public access to information and protect fundamental freedoms, in accordance with national legislation and international agreements.
  • STRENGTHEN NATIONAL INSTITUTIONS TO PREVENT VIOLENCE AND COMBAT TERRORISM AND CRIME
    Strengthen relevant national institutions, including through international cooperation, for building capacity at all levels, in particular in developing countries, to prevent violence and combat terrorism and crime.
  • PROMOTE THE RULE OF LAW AND ENSURE EQUAL ACCESS TO JUSTICE
    Promote the rule of law at the national and international levels and ensure equal access to justice for all
  • REDUCE VIOLENCE EVERYWHERE
    Significantly reduce all forms of violence and related death rates everywhere.
  • SUBSTANTIALLY REDUCE CORRUPTION AND BRIBERYb
    Substantially reduce corruption and bribery in all their forms.
